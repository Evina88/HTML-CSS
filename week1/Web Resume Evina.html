Learning strategies

The learning strategies that I found most helpful, because I was unaware of them, where these:

  • The Spaced Practise

    • It actually seems that there is no other way to learn efficiently. Studying for many hours straight doesn't guarantee you will learn as much as you think. And since right now I have a lot in my plate I was forced to make an effective program to manage all my obligations. Bit by bit, day by day I am able to learn and remember more things now than 2 weeks ago.

  • Switch

    • Switching subjects is one of my favourite practises. Whenever I get bored of studying something, I switch to some exercises or to the next subject I feel I haven't yet mastered.
